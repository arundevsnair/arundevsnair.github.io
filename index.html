Why Choose Us

Whether you are a beginner or have been practicing yoga for years, come and discover all the benefits yoga has to offer. Registration

In order to secure a spot in a class, pre-registration and pre-payment is required. Pre-registration will determine if a class will be held or cancelled. If you are interested in a class; please register as soon as possible or at least 5 days before the class is scheduled to begin.
